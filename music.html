Music

Photo: Cassandra at the Columbia Pops Orchestra's Fall 2019 Concert

Outside of computer science, mathematics, and technology, I am a cellist and eager participant in the musical community at Columbia. I serve as VP Finance (and formerly VP Social) and Principal Cellist of the Columbia Pops Orchestra, where I organize internal events and fundraisers and work on fostering an inclusive community. I also play cello in the Columbia University Orchestra and in a piano trio.

---

I have been playing cello for thirteen years. It is an incredible creative outlet. My experiences with the cello also have made me more courageous, empathetic, and a more effective leader. From playing at Carnegie Hall and performing at the local library in front of all of my friends and family, to exploring the depths of both happiness and sadness as I learn Schumann's cello concerto, to determining the bowings for the Columbia Pops cello section, my music constantly drives my personal growth.
